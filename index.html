The Beginning

Choosing the Yarn

Every project starts with yarn I'd actually want to touch. Merino, alpaca, cotton blends — I won't use anything scratchy or thin.

The Making

Knit by Hand

No machines. Every stitch is placed by hand in my Pennsylvania home studio, usually with a podcast on and a dog at my feet.

Pennsylvania Studio
Meet Molly
"I wanted to make things that outlast the trend they were bought in."

Every stitch is placed by hand in my Pennsylvania home studio. What started as a quiet hobby during a cold winter turned into something I couldn't put down — and now it's how I spend my days.

I make pieces that are meant to be used, washed, handed down, and remembered. Not just admired. Every blanket, every scarf, every tiny baby bootie comes from the same place: a genuine love for making things with my hands.

Custom orders are my favorite part of this work. Tell me what you're looking for and let's figure out what we can make together.

— Molly
